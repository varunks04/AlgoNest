# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility and apologizing to those affected by mistakes

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards and
may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and other contributions
that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.
